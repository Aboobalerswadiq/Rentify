Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information while using Rentify.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Rentify ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process and protect information when you use the Rentify platform, including the Customer App, Vendor App and our website. This policy applies to all users, including customers, vendors and visitors.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Rentify, we may collect account information, contact details, booking information, payment-related records, device information, location information (with your permission), and communication history. Vendors may also provide business information, verification documents, equipment details, pricing, availability and images required to create and manage rental listings.

3. Permissions We Request

Rentify may request access to your Camera, Photos/Storage and Location only when required for features such as uploading documents, equipment images, profile photos and providing location-based rental services. These permissions can be managed through your device settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us create and manage accounts, verify vendors, review equipment listings, process bookings and payments, facilitate customer and vendor communication, improve platform performance, prevent fraud, maintain security and comply with legal obligations.

5. Vendor Verification & Equipment Listing Review

Vendor registrations, submitted documents and equipment listings may be reviewed before approval. During the review process, Rentify may verify business information, review listing details and apply applicable platform commissions before listings become available to customers.

6. Booking & Payments

Booking information is shared only with the parties involved in fulfilling the rental. Online payments may be processed through authorized payment providers. Rentify does not intentionally store complete payment card information.

7. Notifications

We may send notifications related to account updates, booking requests, approvals, payments, security alerts and important platform announcements.

8. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with customers, vendors, authorized administrators, payment providers, cloud hosting providers and authorities where required by law.

9. Third-Party Services

Rentify may use trusted third-party providers for payment processing, cloud hosting, analytics, notifications and technical support solely to operate and improve the platform.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical and organizational safeguards including secure storage, encrypted communications, authentication mechanisms and controlled access to protect your information.

11. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to manage accounts, process bookings, satisfy legal obligations, prevent fraud and support operational requirements.

12. Your Privacy Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may request access to, correction of or deletion of your personal information, withdraw certain permissions and contact us regarding privacy concerns.

13. International Operations

Your information may be processed or stored in locations where Rentify or its service providers operate, subject to applicable laws and appropriate safeguards.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be made available through the platform and continued use of Rentify constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact our support team.

16. Acceptance

By creating an account, registering as a vendor, listing equipment, making bookings or otherwise using the Rentify platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood and agree to this Privacy Policy.
